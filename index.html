you will find below:
A series of unofficial concept ads and creatives for different brands, creatively directed by me and visualized using AI-generated scenes.

Redbull – Unleash the beast

"The ad begins with a bull running energetically into a wrestling arena. It jumps into the ring and transforms into a wrestler holding a can of Red Bull, shouting: Unleash the beast. The ad highlights how Red Bull brings out the energy and power within you."

Burger king - The Burger invasion has begun

"The ad shows people running from giant burger sandwiches falling from the sky — as if being dropped like bombs. Tagline: “The burger invasion has begun.” The ad uses humor and exaggeration to match current global themes, presenting Burger King’s arrival as an unstoppable force."

Fly Emirates - Relax

"The ad opens with a man relaxing on a bed, looking completely calm and ready to sleep. He turns off the bedside lamp, and as the camera pans to the window, we realize — he’s not in a bedroom, but inside an Emirates airplane. The ad conveys the luxurious comfort you experience flying with Emirates."

Don't try with BMW

"The ad begins with a BMW M5 G90 speeding along an open road. A peregrine falcon — the fastest creature on Earth — spots the car and tries to race it. It flies at top speed, but eventually gives up and pulls away, leaving the BMW to continue on alone. Tagline: “Don’t even try with BMW.” The ad emphasizes the unmatched power of BMW — even nature’s fastest creature surrenders to it."

Monster - Grab the energy

"The ad starts with the iconic green mask from the movie "The Mask" placed beside a can of Monster. A person drinks from the can, puts on the mask, and transforms into the cartoonish, hyperactive version of The Mask. The message: the real source of the wild character isn't the mask — it's the Monster drink."

Domino's Pizza - Don't be surprised

"A customer picks up their order at a Domino’s store, but suddenly notices someone unexpected — Michelangelo from Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles — sitting and happily eating Domino’s pizza. Tagline: “Don’t be surprised. You’re at Domino’s.” The ad implies Domino’s is so good, even the most pizza-obsessed characters in pop culture choose it."

Nike - Not from this world

"A spaceship lands on a school football field. A mysterious alien steps out and leaves something behind. As the camera zooms in, it reveals a brand-new pair of Nike shoes. Tagline: “Nike – Not from this world.” The ad positions Nike as futuristic and otherworldly, suggesting its performance and design come from beyond Earth."
